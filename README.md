# Dashboard-of-Vietnam-University-Exam-and-Admission-Score
This project analyzes Vietnam’s National High School Exam scores (2022–2023) and university admission cutoff scores (2018–2023). Using cleaned, crawled data and interactive dashboards, it provides clear insights into score distributions, admission trends, and helps students choose suitable universities and majors.

## Purpose / Benefits:
- Provide an overview of Vietnam National High School Exam (THPTQG) scores by subject and subject combinations from 2022–2023.
- Provide insights into university admission cutoff scores across different admission methods in Vietnam from 2018–2023.
- Help users better understand score distributions and make informed decisions when choosing universities and majors.

## Stakeholders:
- High school students
- Parents and teachers
- Individuals interested in Vietnam’s university admission process

## Results:
- Crawled dataset of THPT National Exam scores (2022–2023) and University admission cutoff scores (2018–2023)
- Cleaned and processed data
- Built interactive dashboards:
   #### 1. Exam Score Dashboard highlights:
    - Number of exam candidates nationwide and by province; subject-level statistics and key subject-combination statistics
    - Provincial rankings by average subject or combination scores
    - Counts of candidates below passing thresholds, below average, and high achievers
  #### 2. University Admission Cutoff Dashboard highlights
    - Overview of average, highest, and lowest cutoff scores by field, subfield, and major
    - Universities with the highest and lowest cutoffs by major
    - University profiles: geographic location and institution type (public/private)
    - Personalized recommendations for suitable universities and majors based on user scores
